# HCL/Citibank AU - Diners Cards OASIS Migration
**Role:** Software Engineer / Lead Software Engineer  
**Platform:** OpenText Captiva InputAccal 5.x/6.x · Documentum 5.x/6.x  
**Parent Organization:** HCL Technologies (Singapore Hub)

---

## Overview

Diners Cards had been integrated into the Citi card business. Citibank wanted to transition various Diner's workflows — including those of the Merchant Service Team, Service Management Team, and Customer Service Team — to its existing platform, OASIS. Data segregation and security protocols were needed to separate Citibank's OASIS BAU data from Diners' data.

---

## Tech Stack

- **OpenText:** Documentum 5.3/6.x (Content Server, DA, Composer, Webtop/WDK, DFC, BPM), Captiva InputAccal 5.x/6.x (Administrator Console, ScanPlus, IndexPlus, Image Enhancement, Multi, ODBC Export, Documentum Export, eInput/eScan/eIndex, Dispatcher, ScaleServer)
- **Infrastructure:** MSCS Active/Passive, SQL Server 2005/2008, Windows Server 2003/2008, IIS 6/7, Oracle 9i/11g
- **Languages:** C#.NET, VB6, Java, VBA

---

## Key Focus Areas

- **Merchant Service Team Workflows** — Transition merchant processing workflows to OASIS
- **Service Management Team Workflows** — Migrate service management processes
- **Customer Service Team Workflows** — Convert customer service operations to unified platform

---

## Key Achievements

- **Business Integration:** Successfully integrated Diners Cards operations into Citibank's consolidated platform
- **Data Migration:** Migrated all Diners workflows and associated documents
- **Data Segregation:** Implemented strict data segregation protocols separating Citibank's OASIS BAU data from Diners' sensitive data
- **Security Protocols:** Established security controls, access management, and audit trails for segregated data
- **CaptureFlow Development:** Created Diners-specific Captiva workflows adapted from OASIS platform
- **Backend Integration:** Built ODBC and SOAP integrations for data validation and processing
- **Team Training:** Provided training and support to Merchant Service, Service Management, and Customer Service teams
- **Production Transition:** Managed cutover from legacy Diners systems to unified OASIS platform

---

## Impact

Successfully consolidated Diners Cards workflows into Citibank's OASIS platform while maintaining strict data segregation and security protocols, enabling unified management of Citi card services across both product lines.
