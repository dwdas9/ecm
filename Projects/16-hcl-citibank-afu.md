# HCL/Citibank - Project AFU (Enterprise Content Management)
**Role:** Software Engineer / Lead Software Engineer  
**Platform:** OpenText Captiva InputAccal 5.x/6.x · Documentum 5.x/6.x  
**Parent Organization:** HCL Technologies (Singapore Hub)
**Geographic Scope:** 40+ Branches Across India

---

## Overview

Project AFU aims at implementing enterprise content management functionality for Citibank major business lines — banking, insurance, and mortgage. Business Challenge: Implementing document capture solution at the bank's 40+ branches spread across India. Processing and validating the captured documents and converting them into enterprise-ready content. Feeding the content to back-end ECM system. Applying ECM functionalities like security, version control, content lifecycles, workflows, categorization, and search to the stored content.
